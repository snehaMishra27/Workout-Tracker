About AlphaBurn

⚡AlphaBurn is a powerful yet beautifully simple fitness tracking web app built by Sneha. It helps users stay consistent, track their workouts, monitor health metrics, and stay motivated through personalized progress visualizations.


Whether you're a beginner starting your fitness journey or someone chasing bigger goals, AlphaBurn is designed to keep you on track — with features like workout logging, health tracking (weight, heart rate, sleep), real-time dashboards, and weekly goal monitoring.


Developed with a clean UI and dynamic insights, AlphaBurn empowers you to love your body, track your journey, and live healthier every day. 🌟
